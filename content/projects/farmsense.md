\## Overview



FarmSense is an AI-powered precision agriculture platform designed to help farmers make smarter, data-driven decisions. The platform enables users to manage agricultural fields, monitor crop health, track irrigation and fertilizer usage, and receive intelligent recommendations through an integrated AI assistant.



\## The Challenge



Traditional farm management often relies on manual record keeping and delayed decision-making. FarmSense addresses these challenges by combining modern web technologies with artificial intelligence to provide real-time insights into crop health, farm operations, and resource utilization.



\## Key Features



\- AI-powered crop disease detection using computer vision

\- Intelligent conversational farm assistant

\- Field and crop lifecycle management

\- Irrigation and fertilizer tracking

\- Interactive dashboard with farm analytics

\- Secure authentication and role-based access

\- Historical crop monitoring and record keeping



\## AI Capabilities



The platform integrates computer vision and large language models to assist farmers throughout the growing cycle. Farmers can upload crop images for disease analysis and interact with an AI assistant that provides context-aware recommendations based on crop history, irrigation records, and fertilizer usage.



\## Outcome



FarmSense demonstrates how artificial intelligence can modernize agriculture by combining farm management, computer vision, and conversational AI into a unified platform. The project showcases full-stack engineering, AI integration, and scalable system design for real-world agricultural applications.
